Our Story

Craft Meets Passion

Lumière was born in 2016 from a simple belief: that every person deserves to feel beautiful, seen, and celebrated. We built a space in the heart of Nairobi that blends world-class technique with genuine warmth.

Our team of 12 stylists and therapists train continuously at leading academies in Nairobi, London, and Johannesburg — so you always receive the very best.
